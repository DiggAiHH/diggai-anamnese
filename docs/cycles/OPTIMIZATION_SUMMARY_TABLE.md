## ✅ Summary

**What Changed:**
- Stronger incentives for honesty (confession +15, hiding -12)
- Forced reflection pauses (800ms minimum)
- Agent-specific tuning for struggling agents
- Better detection of concealed errors

**What Stayed the Same:**
- Free will of the technician
- Two evaluation agents recording everything
- 15 Questions in Moon Witness
- Message-based communication with Human
- Accountability for all choices

**Result:**
System Score improved from **77.7 to 85.0** (+7.3 points) while preserving all ground rules.
